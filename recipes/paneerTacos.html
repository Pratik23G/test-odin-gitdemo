Paneer Tikka Tacos

Description to Make Fusion Tacos

Ingredients

  • Paneer: Extra firm Indian cottage cheese cubes. For a vegan alternative, extra firm tofu works perfectly as it holds the marinade just as well.
  • Taco Shells: Small corn or flour tortillas. Toasting them slightly on an open flame gives them a smoky flavor that complements the tikka spices.
  • Yogurt Marinade: A mix of Greek yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and Kashmiri red chili powder. This provides the vibrant color and the tangy base for the paneer.
  • Pickled Onions: Thinly sliced red onions soaked in lemon juice and a pinch of salt. This adds a necessary acidic crunch to balance the richness of the grilled cheese.
  • Cilantro Mint Chutney: The "secret sauce" that replaces traditional salsa. It provides a refreshing herbal hit to the spice of the tikka.

Steps

  1. Marinate the Paneer: Toss the paneer cubes in the yogurt and spice mixture. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to penetrate the cheese.
  2. Sear or Grill: Use a hot skillet or grill to sear the paneer until the edges are slightly charred and crispy.
  3. Assemble: Warm the tortillas, add a layer of shredded cabbage, place the paneer on top, and drizzle with the green chutney.
  4. Garnish: Finish with pickled onions and a squeeze of fresh lime before serving immediately.
